Introduction

This online booklet follows the footsteps of Henryk Bankier in Warsaw, blending family memoir notes with historical context and practical city highlights. Use the tables to browse each tour’s stops, and scroll the interactive maps to orient yourself.

A loop through Henryk’s early residences and workplaces around Grzybowska & Grzybowski Square.
